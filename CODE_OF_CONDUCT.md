# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical concerns.
- Keep discussions focused on the SDK, protocol behavior, documentation, and operations.
- Avoid sharing private registry credentials, certificates, or sensitive EPP payloads.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Public disclosure of private credentials, keys, authInfo values, or sensitive registry data.
- Sustained disruption of technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in being blocked from participation.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the project maintainer.
